The Lazarus Group, believed to be affiliated with North Korea, has launched a campaign targeting financial institutions. 
The attack utilizes Andariel malware, known for its capabilities in financial theft. 
The threat actor exploited an unpatched VPN vulnerability to gain initial access. 
The malware was delivered through a watering hole attack on a popular finance-related website. 
Communication was observed with the IP address 198.51.100.22 and domain securebank-payments.net.
